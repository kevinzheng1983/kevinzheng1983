Short Biography | 中文简介
Dr. Kai Zheng is a full professor with University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). He is also a senior scientist and founding member of PCHAIN, the first native multichain system that supports EVM. He received Ph.D. (in 2012), Master (in 2009) and Bachelor (in 2006) degrees of Computer Science from The University of Queensland (UQ) (advised by Prof Xiaofang Zhou), Fudan University (advised by Prof Aoying Zhou) and Tongji University, respectively. Before joining UESTC, he has worked as Research Fellow (2012 - 2013) and ARC DECRA (Australian Research Council Discovery Early Career Research Award) Fellow (2014 - 2016) with the Data and Knowledge Engineering Group of UQ. His research interests and expertise cover the full stack of data management technologies including data storage, clearning, indexing and search, as well as many other challenging data intensive problems. He received a few best paper awards on international conferences including ICDE 2019 and 2015, APWeb-Waim 2017, WISE 2017. He served the General co-Chair of DASFAA 2017, Program co-Chair of APWeb 2016, and Program Committee for over 20 top-tier conferences including SIGMOD, PVLDB, ICDE, KDD, IJCAI, AAAI, WWW, etc. He is the editor of the Journal of Distributed and Parallel Databases (TAPD) and the journal of Data & Knowledge Engineering (DKE), the guest editor for the Journal of WWW, Geoinformatica and Frontier of Computer Science. He has published over 120 peer-reviewed papers in top-tier conferences and journals in the area of Big Data and AI.
